

# Personal Website for Academic & Tech Leader

## Overview
A modern, minimalist personal website showcasing your dual role as Group CTO at Kifiya Financial Technology and Assistant Professor at Addis Ababa University. Clean lines, elegant typography, and a professional aesthetic.

## Pages & Sections

### 1. Hero / Landing
- Full-width hero with your professional photo, name, and titles
- Brief tagline about your focus on AI, FinTech, and financial inclusion
- Smooth scroll navigation

### 2. About
- Your professional summary highlighting AI/ML expertise, financial technology, and commitment to financial inclusion
- Clean two-column layout with key focus areas (AI, FinTech, Credit Scoring, Financial Inclusion)

### 3. Experience Timeline
- Elegant vertical timeline showing your career journey:
  - Group CTO @ Kifiya (2025–Present)
  - CAIO @ Kifiya (2024–Present)
  - Head of Credit Research @ Kifiya (2024)
  - Assistant Professor @ Addis Ababa University (2011–Present)
  - CTO @ iCog Labs (2016–2018)
  - Senior PM, Sophia Robot @ Hanson Robotics (2016–2018)

### 4. Teaching & Courses
- Organized by level (BSc, MSc, PhD) using tabs or accordion
- Each course shown as a compact card with title and one-line description
- Covers your full curriculum from Intro to Programming through Advanced Computer Vision

### 5. Education
- Clean cards for PhD (ENIB), MSc and BSc (Addis Ababa University)

### 6. Contact / Footer
- Professional contact section with links to LinkedIn, email, etc.
- Simple, elegant footer

## Design Direction
- **Style**: Modern minimalist with generous whitespace and clean lines
- **Colors**: Dark navy/charcoal primary with subtle accent color
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts, strong hierarchy
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in on scroll for sections
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly
- **Single page**: Smooth-scrolling one-page layout with fixed top navigation
