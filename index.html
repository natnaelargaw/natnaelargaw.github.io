About Me

A seasoned Computer Scientist with a PhD in Computer Science and Digital Architecture from the prestigious National Engineering School of Brest, France. My 13-year career trajectory spans from foundational programming to executive leadership, culminating in roles such as COO/CTO/CAIO at Kifiya Financial Technology. With a proven track record in driving innovation and redefining technological landscapes, I've also contributed to academia as an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Addis Ababa University. My expertise encompasses a broad spectrum of roles, from spearheading data science research, model validation, and strategy to leading AI initiatives.
