About

With a strong academic foundation from Addis Ababa University, I have honed my expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Financial Technology, focusing on credit scoring, alternative data analytics, AI-driven risk assessment, and financial inclusion. My academic and research background has been instrumental in shaping data-driven financial innovations that cater to MSMEs, underserved populations, and financially excluded communities. At Kifiya, I lead the development of next-generation fintech infrastructure, leveraging deep learning, cloud computing, and Web 3.0 to pioneer alternative credit scoring models and agentic systems. I am deeply committed to bridging the gap between technology and financial accessibility, ensuring AI-driven financial services are both inclusive and impactful.
