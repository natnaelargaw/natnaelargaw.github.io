Greetings!

I am Natnael Argaw Wondimu, a seasoned professional with a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology and contributing to the ever-evolving field of computer science. Holding a PhD in computer science and digital architectures from the prestigious Brest National School of Engineering in France, my journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of knowledge and a commitment to excellence.

Academic Prowess

My academic journey has been adorned with noteworthy achievements, including the publication of cutting-edge research findings in renowned conference proceedings like ICAART and esteemed journals such as Springer Nature. With over 12 years of combined industry and academic experience, I currently serve as an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Addis Ababa University, where I am dedicated to shaping the minds of the next generation of innovators.

Professional Experience

My professional journey is a testament to my diverse skill set and leadership capabilities. I have had the privilege of serving as the Dean of Addis Ababa Institute of Technology School of Information Technology and Engineering for three years, where I played a pivotal role in shaping the academic landscape. As the Project Manager at Hanson Robotics for four years, I led teams to bring cutting-edge projects to life, contributing to the forefront of technological innovation. In the realm of startups, I served as the Chief Operational Officer at iCog Labs, an AI startup company, where I harnessed my entrepreneurial spirit to drive success. My experience also extends to large-scale IT projects, having worked with industry giants such as Hershey's and MedAlert. Notably, I am the ERP consultant for the Ethiopia Sugar Industry group, leveraging my expertise to streamline operations and drive efficiency.

Technical Expertise

As a certified Oracle Database 12C/19C/21C administrator, I bring a wealth of technical expertise to the table. My proficiency extends beyond academia and into the practical realm, where I have actively contributed to the success of various IT projects, leaving an indelible mark on the organizations I have had the privilege to collaborate with.

Join the Journey

Embark on this journey with me as we explore the realms of technology, academia, and innovation. Whether you're a fellow enthusiast, a student eager to learn, or a company seeking a trusted advisor, I invite you to connect, collaborate, and be part of a community dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the world of computer science. Welcome to a world where expertise meets innovation, and together, we shape the future!
